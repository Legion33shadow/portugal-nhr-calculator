FAQ

What is the NHR regime in Portugal?

Non-Habitual Resident status grants a flat 20% tax rate on Portuguese-source income and exemptions on foreign income for 10 years. New applicants from 2024 use IFICI instead.

What is the difference between NHR and IFICI?

NHR closed to new applicants after December 2023. IFICI (D8 compatible) applies a 20% flat rate for 10 years to new registrants from 2024 onwards.

Can digital nomads benefit from NHR IFICI?

Yes. The D8 Digital Nomad Visa is compatible with IFICI. You need to prove remote income above Portuguese minimum wage and register as a tax resident within 12 months.
